## Color-coding key

- 🟢 on track — no leader attention needed
- 🟡 at risk — surface in weekly check-in
- 🔴 blocked — flag immediately, propose intervention
- ✅ already met — celebrate, then redirect attention

## How to surface this in a board update

3 wins (pick the strongest 🟢/✅), 3 challenges (the 🔴s + the 🟡 with
the biggest revenue / org impact), 1 ask (what the leader needs from
the board). Never just dump the OKR list.
